Every feature built from first principles. No frameworks. No abstractions. Just metal.

Audio

Full-duplex with hardware AEC.

CoreAudio VoiceProcessingIO with acoustic echo cancellation. Lock-free ring buffers on the real-time thread. Barge-in interrupts playback instantly.

Intelligence

Three signals. One decision.

Fused end-of-utterance detection: energy VAD, LSTM endpointer, and ASR token — weighted and smoothed for sub-240ms turn detection. Speculative prefill at 70% confidence.

Streaming

Sentence by sentence.

Claude responses stream via SSE. Smart sentence buffering with predictive flush feeds TTS incrementally. SSML prosody, breath synthesis, and LUFS normalization on every chunk.

Quality

Proven, not promised.

Native C quality framework: WER, CER, MCD, STOI, SNR, F0 analysis, and speaker similarity. Round-trip testing proves end-to-end intelligibility.
